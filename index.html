About Us

Kitimat Sikh Society is a place of worship and community gathering for Sikhs in Kitimat, BC. We are committed to the principles of equality, service, and devotion.

Our History

Founded over 20 years ago, the Society has grown into a spiritual and cultural hub for Sikh families in the region. We have worked with the community as well as other charitable trusts for the growth of the city as well as the Humanity.

Services

  • Daily Path in the morning and evenings
  • Sunday Langar and Satsang from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM
  • Gurbani and Kirtan Classes avilable on request
  • Community Support Events - Volleyball arena by the temple
